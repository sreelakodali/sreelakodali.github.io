Sreela Kodali, Ph.D.
Electrical Engineer,
Researcher, & Creative

I'm a wearables, robotics, & perception researcher. I design devices to improve health and daily life.

I recently completed my Ph.D. in electrical engineering at Stanford University where I collaborated with the National Institutes of Health (NIH) to characterize touch perception and develop wearable sensory prostheses. My rigorous technical training and interdisciplinary research experience uniquely enables me to tackle challenging, multidisciplinary problems. I’m interested in a broad range of topics including health applications, wearable / embedded systems, medical devices, firmware development, hardware-software co-design, perception, human factors design, UX, assistive technology, IoT and AI-powered devices, open-source development, ASIC and SoC design, robotics, technology policy, STEM education, fashion, politics, film, community organizing.
